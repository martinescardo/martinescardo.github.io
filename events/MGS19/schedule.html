Each course consists of lectures and exercise classes.

The rooms UG06, UG07 (UG05 on Tuesday), and UG09 are all in the Learning Centre opposite Computer Science.

On Sunday we will start 30 minutes later to allow people to take the train from the city centre to the University.

The student presentations will be on Monday evening, starting at 6:30pm, in the Sloman Lounge adjacent to the CS Atrium.

The conference dinner will be on Wednesday evening, starting at 7:30pm, at Pasta di Piazza in Birmingham's Jewellery Quarter. Walking directions, kindly provided by Paul Taylor. Check your menu choices here.
