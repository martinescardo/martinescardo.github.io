Access to the wireless network at MGS 2008

You can contact in two ways to the wireless network:
  • through eduroam: If you are from a UK university which has enabled the eduroam - service by Janet, you may use your eduroam-credentials and log in to the wireless network with the essid "eduroam".
  • through the guest network: if you are unable to use eduroam, you need an account on the wireless network with the essid "UOBGUEST". We have created these accounts if your home University is not connected to eduroam, and will distribute them during registration.
