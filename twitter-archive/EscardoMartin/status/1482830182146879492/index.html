@sarah_zrf @lessgradedalg There is a stronger notion that is also useful: a type X is totally separated if whenever p(x)=p(y) for all p:X->2, then x=y. Then are closed under Pi but not Sigma. The binary sequences are totally separated, but the reals are not (necessarily) totally separated.
