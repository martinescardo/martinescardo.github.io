@XenaProject @plt_abbie (5) Equality of objects is not evil. Only set-theoretical equality is. Equality of objects in HoTT/UF is sublime. And so is equality of categories.
Equality of objects coincides with isomorphism, and equality of categories coincides with equivalence. These are theorems of HoTT.
