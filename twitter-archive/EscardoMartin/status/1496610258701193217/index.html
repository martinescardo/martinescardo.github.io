Martin Escardo

@EscardoMartin

There are two ways of doing this.
(i) We reconstruct it at the same time explaining everything we did. (This is more laborious.)
(ii) We just reconstruct it by asking Agda, at each stage, to simplify the next goal. (This is quicker but leads to proofs which are unreadable.)
