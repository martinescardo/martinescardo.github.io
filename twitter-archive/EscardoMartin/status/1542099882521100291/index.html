@XenaProject @andrejbauer Also, it is important that " X ≃ Y " doesn't say "X and Y are equivalent". The type X ≃ Y collects *all* equivalences from X to Y, where "equivalence" is the refinement of the notion of isomorphism that works for all (homotopy) types, not just those that are sets.
