We have
1. ∃ as in the BHK interpretation, which amounts to essentially Σ.
2. Σ and ∃ as in a 1-topos.
3. Σ as in Martin Löf type theory (MLTT). There ∃ is defined to be Σ.
4. Σ and ∃ as in the calculus of inductive constructions (CiC).
5. Σ and ∃ as in HoTT/UF.
