(6) LPFT version 2: If a map r: A ‚Üí (A ‚Üí X) is a surjection, then every map f: X ‚Üí X has a fixed point.
This is more and less general than (5) .
More: Retractions are surjections.
Less: The notion of surjection can't be formulated in type theory without HoTT extensions.
