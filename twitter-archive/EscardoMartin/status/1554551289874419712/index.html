@johncarlosbaez @plain_simon It is easy to say what the KK spaces are to a classical mathematician: they are the spaces that you obtain from iterating function spaces starting from the discrete natural numbers. In the first step you get N^N, the Baire space, in the next step you get N^(N^N), and so on.
