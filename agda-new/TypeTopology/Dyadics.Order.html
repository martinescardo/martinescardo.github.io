1) x * pos (2^ b) < y * pos (2^ a)
2) y * pos (2^ c) < z * pos (2^ b)

Multiplication of 1) by pos (2^ c)
                  2) by pos (2^ a)

gives that x * pos (2^ b) * pos (2^ c)
