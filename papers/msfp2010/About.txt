These are companion Haskell and Agda files for the paper
MSFP'2010 paper by Martin Escardo & Paulo Oliva

  What Sequential Games, the Tychonoff Theorem and the Double-Negation
  Shift have in Common

Free to anybody to use with usual academic citation.

The zip file in this directory is intended to hold the complete tree
restricted to the haskell and agda programs, and About.txt's.
